The loan agreement is between you and the partner lender you select. We earn a referral fee from lenders upon successful disbursement; we never charge customers a fee.
No. The eligibility check on MyCashBridge is a soft enquiry only. Soft enquiries are visible only to you and do not affect your CIBIL or Experian credit score in any way. A hard enquiry is only initiated by the partner lender after you formally apply.
You can apply for a personal loan ranging from ₹10,000 to ₹3,00,000. The actual eligible amount depends on your monthly income, credit score, employment type, and the individual lending policies of the partner lender.
Interest rates range from 18% to 42% per annum. The APR (including interest plus fees) ranges from 20% to 70% depending on the lender and your credit profile. Always compare APR across offers for a true cost comparison.
For pre-approved profiles with complete KYC and documents, partner lenders disburse funds in 30 minutes to 2 hours after loan approval. The actual timeline depends on the lender’s verification process and your bank’s clearing cycle.
No documents needed to check eligibility or see offers. To formally apply with a lender you will need: Aadhaar, PAN, last 3 months’ salary slips (salaried) or last 2 years’ ITR (self-employed), last 6 months’ bank statements, and a passport-size photograph.
